# Boundaries

### <font style="color:red">Red</font>, not to be depicted in game

- Harm/abuse to children
- PC-PC Romance
- Sex between PCs
- Emotional abuse between PCs
- Physical abuse (eg. extended torture of innocents)

	- Between PCs
